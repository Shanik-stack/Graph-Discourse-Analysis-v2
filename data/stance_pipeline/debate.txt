MOTION: ban private cars in city centers
PRO: Cars cause 40% of urban emissions and worsen air quality in dense areas.
CON: Banning cars harms disabled people who rely on private transport and will reduce access.
PRO: Electric buses solve capacity problems because they're efficient and reduce emissions.
CON: Your solution is hypothetical since electric buses aren't deployed at scale yet.
PRO: But we agree emissions are urgent—that's the core issue we must address now.
CON: Urgency doesn't justify unworkable bans; we need phased policies instead.
